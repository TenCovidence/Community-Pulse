Executive Summary

This comprehensive report highlights key support metrics, user feedback, and operational insights from July 2024 to June 2025. It details our ongoing commitment to efficient support, identifies areas for product enhancement, and provides a forward-looking perspective on our strategic initiatives.

This report also introduces new insights into invitation and account management challenges, regional B2B ticket patterns, and outlines our upcoming product releases and strategic focus areas.
