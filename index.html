Community Pulse

July 2024 to June 2025 Report

Executive Summary

This report highlights key support metrics, user feedback, and operational insights from July 2024 to June 2025. It details our ongoing commitment to efficient support, identifies areas for product enhancement, and provides a forward-looking perspective on our strategic initiatives.

Over the past year, our GrooveHQ support inbox operations have maintained a high level of efficiency, processing over 25,000 tickets with commendable response and resolution times. User sentiment remains positive, driven by continuous improvements and attentive service. We've identified critical areas through detailed audits, such as specific pain points within our core functionalities, and emerging trends like the increase in DE1-related issues. This report also introduces insights into invitation and account management challenges, regional B2B ticket patterns, and outlines our strategic focus areas.

Top 3 Platform Stage-related Ticket Queries

We analysed our support tickets by platform stage to identify where the highest volumes of queries occur. "Screening" (which includes Title and abstracts screening and Full text review stages) remains the most frequent query, followed closely by "Import" and "DE2", indicating key areas for product refinement and user education.

📈

Emerging Trend to Watch

DE1 tagged tickets are steadily increasing, approaching the top 3 platform stage-related issues. This requires proactive investigation to maintain our high standards of user experience.

Monthly Audit of User Wishlist and Feedback

What our users are asking for
Stakeholders: Product and Engineering, Leadership

💡Feature Requests (234 Total)

🔍

Jul: Improve 'load more' button

Users requested enhanced functionality for the 'load more' button, including sorting and pagination options for easier navigation.

🔍

Aug: Reverse 'most relevant' sorting

A request to reverse the sorting order for 'most relevant' results was noted for improved user preference.

🔍

Sep: Copy DE template to a different review project

Users expressed a need to copy DE templates between different review projects for efficiency.

🔍

Oct: Export to Excel when using DE1 (Live)

A feature to export data into Excel while using the DE1 tool was requested and has since been released.

🔍

Nov: Filter by studies with full text (Live)

The ability to filter studies based on the presence of full text was a user request and is now live.

🔍

Dec: Filter by study language

Users requested a filter option to narrow down studies by their language for more targeted research.

🔍

Jan: Hide journal name/authors during screening

The option to conceal journal names and authors during screening was requested for unbiased review.

🔍

Feb: Turn off auto-detection of duplicates

Users asked for the ability to disable automatic duplicate detection for more manual control.

🔍

Mar: Filter studies by notes

A request was made to allow filtering of studies based on associated notes for easier organization.

🔧DE1 Issues (605 Total)

🔧

Jul-Aug: Incomplete data from RevMan export

Tickets were opened regarding data incompleteness when exporting from RevMan, indicating a need for improved export integrity.

🔧

Sep: Confusion about which DE tool to use

Users expressed confusion regarding the appropriate DE tool to use for their specific tasks.

🔧

Oct: Help with setting up DE template

Requests for assistance in setting up DE templates were common, suggesting a need for clearer guidance.

🔧

Nov: Ability to update from old to new DE1 template

Users desired a seamless process for updating their projects from older DE1 templates to newer versions.

🔧

Dec: Queries about making changes in the template

Users had questions about modifying existing templates, indicating a need for more intuitive editing features.

🔧

Jan: Single reviewer mode (Live)

A request for a single reviewer mode was noted and has since been implemented.

🔧

Feb: Ability to change the judgement

Users required the ability to customise judgement scales to match the various QA templates used across different types of reviews.

🔧

Mar: Start from scratch QA template

The ability to start from scratch again after selecting a QA template was a user need to enhance customizability.

💬General Feedback (164 Total)

💬

Jul: Blind author name during screening

Users suggested the ability to hide author names during screening for unbiased review processes.

💬

Aug: Positive compliments on service

Feedback included positive remarks and compliments regarding the support service provided.

💬

Sep: Restore project to a previous state

Users requested a feature to restore a project to an earlier state or date for recovery purposes.

💬

Oct: Filter by studies that are not tagged

A filter to identify studies without tags was requested to improve organization and tracking.

💬

Nov: Blind author and journal name in screening

Further requests were made to hide both author and journal names during the screening process.

💬

Dec: Queries about merging projects

Users had questions and requests related to the merging of multiple projects.

💬

Jan: Ability to hide reviewers during screening

The option to hide reviewer identities during screening was requested for anonymized processes.

💬

Feb: Find duplicated studies that were removed

Users sought a way to locate and review duplicated studies that had previously been removed.

💬

Mar: Feature to hide comments during screening

A feature to hide comments during screening was requested to streamline the review interface.
